About Us:

Swarna Jayanthi Maha vidyalaya, was established on 1985 in Colombo-Kandy road in the Kegalle district At the very first time there were only 210 students and 5 teacher. Our Vision To emerge as the most efficient and productive School in the district of the Sabaragamuwa province Our Mission To produce a good citizen to the society , each and every child should be equipped with competencies as well as good ethics. So , the subject – oriented and co-curricular activities are developed and implemented accordingly.
